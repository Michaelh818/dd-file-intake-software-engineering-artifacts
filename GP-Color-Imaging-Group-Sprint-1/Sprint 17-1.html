How to use this:

NEW SPRINT
1) Make a copy of this sheet, change X in cell D1 to sprint number.
2) Each team member estimates the number of hours they have to work in the sprint.

ENTERING A NEW TASK
1) For the sprint, select a user story from the product backlog (based on input from Product Owner).
2) Break down the user story into tasks of around 0 to 8 hours.
3) For each task, enter the backlog ID in column B. a description of the task, the originator and responsible team members.
4) Finally, and most Important, enter the initial estimate in hours in column H.

UPDATING
Every day you should re-estimate the tasks entering the new estimate on the next day. This means you could end up with more hours on a task the next day even if you spent time on it in the current day. What is important is to know how much work is left to do. Not how much work was done.
